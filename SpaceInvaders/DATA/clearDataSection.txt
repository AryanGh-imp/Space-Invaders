By selecting this option, you can erase all player data from the system. However, please note the following:

Administrator Password Required:
To proceed with this action, you must enter the Administrator Password. This ensures that only authorized individuals can perform this operation.

Effects of Clearing Data:
All saved player profiles and progress will be permanently deleted.
The leaderboard will be reset, and all player names and scores will be removed.
This action is irreversible.

Restart Required:
After the data has been cleared, the application will automatically close. To continue using the program, you will need to relaunch it manually.
